## The 3-Step Blueprint (Summary)

### Step 1: Target a Boring Market
- Focus on industries with low AI adoption
- Lower competition, higher margins
- Examples: plumbing, law firms, dental, trade businesses
- Use ChatGPT for market research
- Identify pain points through customer interviews
- Research AI tools that solve those pain points

### Step 2: Set Your Gross Margins
- Aim for 80-90% margins
- Set price floor: Cost × 5 = 80% margin
- Productize your offer (no custom builds)
- Scope the outcome clearly
- Monitor margins using AI tools

### Step 3: Pre-Sell Your Solution
- Don't build before you have customers
- Create a one-page offer with:
  - Clear outcome
  - Unique mechanism
  - Scarcity/urgency
  - Risk reversal (guarantee)
- Build a list of 100 prospects
- Cold call/email outreach
- Use 9-step "Rocket Selling System"

---

## The 9-Step Rocket Selling System

1. **Setup** - Set agenda, position as expert
2. **Learn** - Understand customer's business
3. **Decision** - Ask "Why me?" and "Why now?"
4. **Results** - Clarify their desired outcomes
5. **Reality** - Understand current situation
6. **Roadblocks** - Identify what's stopping them
7. **Present Model** - Show your unique methodology
8. **Make Offer** - Match solution to their pain points
9. **The Ask** - Ask for the sale 3 times, BAMF (Book A Meeting From a Meeting)
